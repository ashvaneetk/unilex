  • it helps users to quickly comprehend relations between concepts in large complex classification systems
  • it makes editing SKOS files fast and productive
  • simplifies the process of writing contextual queries for navigation vocabularies
  • imports and exports vocabularies using a variety of sources and formats including SKOS, HTML and Excel
